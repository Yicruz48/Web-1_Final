About me

Hello there!~

My name is Yicel De La Cruz. I am a designer and illustrator born in California and raised in Washington state.

One of the earliest memories as a child was drawing in my brother's library book. I didn't just pick any page to doodle on but the blank pages at the beginning and the end of the book. While I am different from that little girl, I still throw everything at any blank page I see.

I am currently a student designer for Eastern Washington University. I create social media posts for the @dosewu Instagram and have created and animated Instagram stickers for the university. Every day expanding my expertise and abilities.
